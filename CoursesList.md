### If you want a world-class psychology education (recommended order)

1. Stanford: **Robert Sapolsky – Human Behavioral Biology**
2. Yale: **Paul Bloom – Introduction to Psychology**
3. **Crash Course Psychology** (review and reinforcement)
4. **The Science of Well-Being** (Yale)
5. **Learning How to Learn** (Barbara Oakley)
6. **Huberman Lab** (brain, habits, sleep, focus)
7. TED Talks by Brené Brown, Kelly McGonigal, and Jonathan Haidt
8. Read the classic psychology books alongside the lectures.

Following this sequence gives you a strong foundation in neuroscience, cognition, emotion, personality, learning, social behavior, and mental health, while distinguishing well-supported scientific findings from ideas that are mainly of historical interest.
