# Psychology books that changed the world

These had an outsized influence on psychology and public thinking:

1. The Interpretation of Dreams
2. Man's Search for Meaning
3. Thinking, Fast and Slow
4. Influence
5. Behave
6. Emotional Intelligence
7. The Lucifer Effect
8. On Becoming a Person
9. Man and His Symbols
10. Flow
11. The Righteous Mind
12. The Brain That Changes Itself

If your goal is to understand people at a very deep level—from the brain and cognition to personality, society, emotion, and meaning—these 12 books are among the strongest starting points. They combine historical importance with enduring influence, though some classic works (especially Freud's) are also studied today as important historical foundations rather than accepted scientific consensus.
